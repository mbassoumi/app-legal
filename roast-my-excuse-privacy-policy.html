Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Roast My Excuse ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Authentication

We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique anonymous identifier for your device. You may optionally link a Google account to preserve your progress across devices. If you link a Google account, we store your Google display name and email solely for account management purposes.

User-Generated Content

When you submit excuses, appeals, or choose a display name, this content is stored on our servers. Excuses and display names may be visible to other players through the public feed and leaderboards. Please do not include personal information in your submissions.

AI Processing

Your submitted excuses and appeals are processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate scores and feedback. This content is sent to Google's servers for processing. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (including Advertising ID) and usage data to serve ads. We request non-personalized ads only. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Push Notifications

We use Expo Push Notifications to send daily reminders and streak alerts. You can opt out of notifications at any time through the app settings or your device settings. We store push notification tokens solely for delivering notifications you have opted into.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To score and provide AI-generated feedback on your excuses
  • To maintain leaderboards, feeds, and game progress
  • To send push notifications you have opted into
  • To display advertisements
  • To detect and prevent abuse or cheating

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above (Firebase, Google AdMob, Google Gemini AI) solely for the purposes described in this policy. Your excuses and display name are visible to other players through the app's public feed and leaderboards.

Data Retention

Game data is retained as long as you have the app installed. Anonymous authentication data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. You can reset your data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Roast My Excuse is a general audience game intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Uninstalling the app will remove all local data from your device. For users in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
